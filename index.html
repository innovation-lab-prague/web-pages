The Lab has everything that you need!

The Innovation Lab Prague is an creative area of Deutsche Boerse Services in Prague. Whenever you need to conduct training, workshop or just creative session with your colleagues, you are on the right place.

Get Started!

Offerings

Workshops

Our equipment is prepared to support your trainings! The area is suitable for larger groups (max. 20 ppl) which makes this place perfect for workshops and training.

Brainstorming

Use the area for creative sessions in groups up to 12. There are a lot of tools to support you.

Meet-ups

Groups of employess can schedule meet-ups on various topics, eg. A.I. & Machine Learning, DevOps, new tools in SW stack and many other topics.

Co-working

Come along and choose a comfortable place. Meet colleagues from different teams and begin your discussions.
